Why Allied Telesis Switches

01

VCStack Resilience

Virtual chassis stacking creates a unified, fault-tolerant switching fabric across up to 8 units. One management plane, zero single points of failure.

02

AMF Plus Automation

Zero-touch provisioning, automated backup and recovery, and seamless firmware management across your entire switching infrastructure.

03

MACSec & FIPS 140-2

Hardware-level encryption on every link. FIPS 140-2 certified for government and regulated industry compliance requirements.

04

AlliedWare Plus OS

A unified operating system across all switches, routers, and firewalls. Consistent CLI, centralized policy, and automated lifecycle management.
