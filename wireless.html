Wireless Innovation

Four technologies that set Allied Telesis apart in enterprise wireless networking

01

Autonomous Wave Control

AI-powered RF optimization that automatically tunes channel selection, power levels, and coverage patterns for the best possible performance in any environment.

02

Hybrid Wi-Fi (World First)

Simultaneous Channel Blanket single-channel and traditional multi-channel architecture enables seamless roaming with zero handoff delay for mobile clients.

03

AlliedWare Plus OS

Enterprise operating system with deep packet inspection, Passpoint hotspot 2.0, OpenRoaming, and centralized management across your entire network.

04

Built-in Branch Routing

Select models integrate a full VPN router with SD-WAN capability, eliminating the need for separate networking hardware at branch locations.
