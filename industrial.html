Why Allied Telesis Industrial

Fanless IP30 Design

Convection-cooled enclosures with IP30 ingress protection. Operates from -40°C to +75°C without fans, moving parts, or air filters -- zero maintenance in dusty, humid environments.

DIN-Rail & Wall Mount

Standard 35mm DIN-rail mounting fits directly into industrial control cabinets. Optional wall-mount brackets for outdoor enclosures and traffic cabinets.

PoE++ (95W per port)

IEEE 802.3bt PoE++ delivers up to 95W per port and 360W total budget. Power PTZ cameras, industrial sensors, wireless APs, and edge compute devices over a single cable.

MACsec Encryption

Hardware-level MACsec (IEEE 802.1AE) encryption on 10G SFP+ uplinks protects data in transit across the industrial network without impacting throughput or latency.
